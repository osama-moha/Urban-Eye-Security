The Security Gap

Cameras fail when they are placed randomly and monitored poorly.

Having cameras is not the same as having security. Poor camera placement leaves blind spots at gates, parking areas, stockrooms, and loading zones. Weak night visibility makes footage useless when incidents happen after dark. If your CCTV is not connected to alarms, monitoring, or response procedures, you may simply be recording your own losses.
