The Security Gap

Cameras fail when they are placed randomly and monitored poorly.

Having cameras is not the same as having security. Poor camera placement leaves blind spots at gates, parking areas, stockrooms, and loading zones. Weak night visibility makes footage useless when incidents happen after dark. If your CCTV is not connected to alarms, monitoring, or response procedures, you may simply be recording your own losses.

The Urban Eye Solution

Plan, detect, verify, and respond.

We design Urban Eye Video Architectures around how people actually move through your property. We identify vulnerable angles, place cameras for useful evidence, and connect visual verification to clear response workflows.

Active Video Capabilities

CCTV should support decisions, not just storage.

Our CCTV work is built around visibility, accountability, and response — not just installing devices.

Strategic Coverage Mapping

We inspect your property and map gates, corridors, parking zones, perimeter walls, stockrooms, cash points, and loading areas so camera placement follows risk — not guesswork.

Night Visibility Planning

We plan lighting, camera angles, and night-view coverage so critical areas remain visible after hours when many incidents actually occur.

AI-Assisted Detection

Where appropriate, we configure intelligent detection zones, virtual tripwires, line crossing, intrusion detection, and loitering alerts to make cameras more proactive.

Visual Verification

Your camera system can support alarm verification, incident review, and response coordination by helping confirm what happened, where it happened, and what action should follow.
