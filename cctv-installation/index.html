The Security Gap

Cameras fail when they are placed randomly and monitored poorly.

Having cameras is not the same as having security. When cameras are installed without a strategic plan, you are left with blind spots at your gates, poor visibility at night, and no clear way to verify an active intrusion. A camera cannot stop a break-in on its own; if it is not integrated with your alarm system and response procedures, you may simply be recording your own losses.

The Urban Eye Solution

Integrated video surveillance. See everything, from anywhere.

Urban Eye treats CCTV as part of a complete security system, not as a standalone product. Your cameras should support your alarms, guards, response steps, evidence needs, and daily property management.

Strategic Coverage

We plan camera locations around critical chokepoints: entry gates, blind spots, parking areas, cash registers, stockrooms, and perimeter movement.

One-App Integration

Where supported, we link camera streams into your Ajax security app so you can view cameras and manage alarm activity from one connected interface.

24/7 NVR Recording

Secure, continuous on-site recording using Hikvision Network Video Recorders helps preserve high-quality evidence when you need it.

Empowered Guarding

CCTV gives your physical guards digital eyes to verify threats safely before approaching risky areas or escalating a situation.

FAQs

Common CCTV questions.

Clear answers help you choose the right system before spending money on cameras, cabling, NVR storage, or installation.

How many CCTV cameras do I need?

It depends on your property’s layout and access points. We determine the exact number during your site survey to avoid overcharging you for unnecessary cameras.

Can I view cameras from my phone?

Yes. We can integrate your CCTV feeds into the Ajax app where supported, or configure dedicated Hikvision viewing software so you can view live video remotely.

Do you inspect the site first?

Always. We do not quote blindly. A site survey ensures your cameras are positioned to capture actual threats, not just empty walls.
