The Corporate Security Gap

Office security often fails where accountability is weakest.

Standard office security often relies on a guard at the lobby and physical keys that can be easily copied or lost. This leaves businesses vulnerable to unauthorized access, equipment theft, weak reception control, and poor after-hours monitoring.

When an alarm simply makes noise, or a camera only records a break-in after the fact, you are left with evidence of loss rather than active prevention.
