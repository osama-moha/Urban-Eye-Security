The Corporate Security Gap

Office security often fails where accountability is weakest.

Many workplaces still depend on physical keys, shared PINs, unmonitored reception areas, and disconnected alarms. This makes it difficult to know who entered sensitive rooms, when they entered, and whether the correct response happened after an incident.

A corporate security plan should not only react after something goes wrong. It should reduce weak points before they become business disruption, data exposure, theft, or staff-safety incidents.

Core Office Integrations

Built around how your workplace actually operates.

We combine access control, CCTV, zoned intrusion detection, fire safety, and monitoring-ready escalation into one practical office protection framework.

01

Role-Based Access Control

Replace copied keys and shared codes with controlled access for staff, visitors, server rooms, accounting areas, records offices, stockrooms, and executive suites. Access rights can be changed when roles change or staff leave.

02

Reception & Visitor Visibility

Position CCTV around reception desks, main entrances, visitor waiting areas, hallways, and access chokepoints so management has clearer visibility without disrupting daily office flow.

03

Zoned Intrusion Protection

Protect sensitive areas independently. For example, the server room, finance office, or records room can remain armed while other staff continue working in open office areas.

04

Emergency & Life-Safety Readiness

Add panic buttons, fire detection, smoke alerts, and monitoring-ready escalation so emergencies are easier to verify, report, and respond to through a clear procedure.
