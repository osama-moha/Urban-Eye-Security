What security services does Urban Eye provide?

Urban Eye provides CCTV installation, Ajax smart alarms, access control, manned guarding, site surveys, and property security planning in Nairobi.

Do I need a site survey before installation?

Yes. A site survey identifies access points, blind spots, lighting gaps, perimeter risks, and the right mix of people, technology, and procedures.

Can Urban Eye combine guards, CCTV, and alarms?

Yes. Urban Eye focuses on integrated security where guarding, CCTV, alarms, access control, documentation, and support work together.
