FAQs

Common questions.

Do you provide guards for estates and offices?

Yes. Guarding can be planned around estates, offices, warehouses, schools, retail sites, and other properties.

What should a guarding assessment include?

It should review gates, visitor flow, patrol routes, guard post instructions, lighting, reporting, and emergency contacts.

Can guarding work with CCTV and alarms?

Yes. Guarding is stronger when connected to cameras, alarm alerts, access control, and reporting procedures.
