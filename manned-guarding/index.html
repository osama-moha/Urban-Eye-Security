FAQs

Common manned guarding questions.

Good guarding is not just about placing someone at a gate. It requires planning, supervision, communication, and clear operating procedures.

Do you provide guards for both residential estates and commercial offices?

Yes. We provide guarding for private homes, apartments, estates, offices, retail shops, warehouses, and other properties. Post instructions and visitor management procedures are customized to the operational needs of each site.

What does a guarding assessment include?

We review your property layout, perimeter condition, blind spots, lighting, existing alarm systems, access routines, visitor movement, and the number of personnel and procedures required to secure the site.

How does your guarding work with CCTV and smart alarms?

CCTV and Ajax smart sensors help detect and verify threats. This information can be relayed to the on-site guard, allowing them to respond to the exact location of the threat safely and effectively.
