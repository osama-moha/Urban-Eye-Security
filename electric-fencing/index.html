The Perimeter Security Gap

A basic fence can create a false sense of security.

Standard electric fences rely on basic voltage that can be shorted, bridged, cut, or bypassed. Worse, when an alarm triggers along a long perimeter at night, guards may not know exactly where the breach happened.

That forces teams to search dark, exposed, and potentially dangerous areas without enough location context. A real perimeter system should show where the attack happened and trigger the right response workflow.

The Urban Eye Solution

Active, intelligent perimeters.

Urban Eye designs perimeter systems that combine electric fencing, tamper detection, zone planning, alarm lighting, CCTV verification, and monitoring-ready escalation. If an intruder attempts to cut, short, climb, or separate wires, the system helps identify the attack zone and supports faster response.
