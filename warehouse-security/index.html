The Security Gap

Massive perimeters and high-bay racking create critical vulnerabilities.

Relying only on physical guards or basic cameras leaves your distribution center exposed. Large industrial perimeters contain major blind spots, and standard systems can struggle around thick walls, dense storage racks, loading bays, and high-value cages. A passive CCTV camera may only record inventory losses. Without an integrated system that connects the perimeter, loading bays, stock rooms, alarms, guards, and response procedures, your warehouse remains reactive instead of protected.

FAQs

Common warehouse security questions.

Warehouse security requires more planning than a standard small-office installation. These answers help you understand the first concerns we review.

Will the wireless signal reach across my entire warehouse?

Ajax Jeweller delivers a secure communication range of up to 2,000 meters in open space. For large logistics centers, ReX 2 radio signal range extenders can help bypass interference from metal racks and concrete.

Can you integrate our existing warehouse gates and roller shutters?

Yes. Using Ajax relay and transmitter modules, we can integrate third-party automatic gates, turnstiles, electronic bolt locks, and related access-control hardware into a smart security ecosystem.

What happens if our facility loses internet or power?

High-risk Ajax hubs can use multiple communication channels such as Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and SIM backup. Backup batteries help support continued operation during power outages.
