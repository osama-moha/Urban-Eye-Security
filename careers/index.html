Urban Eye operates a practical deployment model. We recruit based on client locations, project requirements, and the standards needed to deliver reliable security work.

Field Operations

Security Officers

Guarding, access control, patrols, visitor management, site reporting, and professional client-facing security service.

Needs-only recruitment

Role purpose

Security Officers represent Urban Eye at client sites by protecting people, property, access points, and daily site operations with discipline, alertness, and professional conduct.

Minimum qualifications

  • Age: 24 to 35 years.
  • Physically, mentally, and medically fit.
  • Good eyesight and ability to remain alert during duty.
  • Minimum KCSE mean grade of D+ and above.
  • Ability to read, write, and communicate confidently in English and Kiswahili.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.
  • No criminal record.

Professional expectations

  • Ability to work day or night shifts depending on deployment.
  • Basic report-writing ability.
  • Discipline, punctuality, and neat personal presentation.
  • Ability to follow site instructions and emergency procedures.
  • Previous guarding, access control, reception security, patrol, or CCTV control room experience is an advantage.

Application note

Recruitment is conducted on a needs-only basis depending on client location, deployment requirements, and operational role.

Technical Projects

Installation Technicians

CCTV, access control, alarms, structured cabling, app setup, testing, troubleshooting, and client handover.

Project-based

Role purpose

Installation Technicians support Urban Eye projects by installing, testing, configuring, and handing over security systems with neat, reliable, and professional workmanship.

Minimum qualifications

  • Proven experience installing CCTV, access control, alarm systems, or related security systems.
  • Basic understanding of IP networks, routers, mobile apps, and device integrations.
  • Clean installation standards with neat cabling and proper device placement.
  • Ability to test systems before handover.
  • Ability to explain basic system use to clients.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.

Useful experience

  • CCTV camera installation and NVR setup.
  • Smart alarm systems and mobile app configuration.
  • Access control readers, locks, keypads, and user permissions.
  • Structured cabling and clean trunking work.
  • Basic troubleshooting and maintenance support.

Application note

Technician opportunities are project-based and depend on installation demand, site complexity, project location, and required technical skill.

Sales & Advisory

Security Solutions Consultants

Technical sales, site surveys, client advisory, proposal support, follow-up, and security framework explanation.

Performance-driven

Role purpose

Security Solutions Consultants help clients understand their risks, identify practical security gaps, and choose the right combination of guarding, CCTV, alarms, access control, monitoring, and response.

Minimum qualifications

  • Proven B2B, real estate, security, facilities, or technical sales experience.
  • Ability to generate leads and follow up professionally.
  • Ability to learn and explain CCTV, alarms, access control, monitoring, and guarding frameworks.
  • Confidence conducting physical site surveys and identifying security gaps.
  • Strong communication, proposal, and client relationship skills.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.

Best fit

  • People comfortable selling consultative security solutions, not just products.
  • Applicants with networks in real estate, facilities, retail, construction, logistics, or property management.
  • People who can explain risks clearly and recommend practical protection plans.

Application note

Consultant roles may be commission-based, performance-based, or project-linked depending on business needs and growth opportunities.
