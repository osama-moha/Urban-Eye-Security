Recruitment Model

How we build our team.

Urban Eye operates a practical deployment model. We recruit based on client locations, project requirements, and the standards needed to deliver reliable security work.

Security Officers Recruitment is conducted on a needs-only basis and is tied to specific client locations. We do not hold mass general recruitments.
Installation Technicians Technical deployment work may be project-based depending on site requirements, installation scope, and client demand.
Security Solutions Consultants Sales and consulting roles operate on a performance-driven structure for people who can generate leads, assess sites, and close security opportunities.

Working With Urban Eye

We expect professionalism, and we give it back.

Urban Eye looks for people who can represent the company with discipline, neatness, confidence, and respect for the client. In return, we provide structured internal training, professional deployment expectations, clear supervision, practical tools, and a performance culture where reliability matters.

Whether you are posted at a residential estate, a commercial property, a warehouse, a construction site, or a technical installation project, your work must support one goal: helping the client feel protected, informed, and properly served.

Open Roles

Roles we recruit for.

Applications are reviewed based on business needs, client deployment requirements, project availability, and candidate suitability.

Role
Status
Minimum qualifications
Security Officers Guarding, access control, patrols, visitor management, and site reporting.
Recruited on a needs-only basis per client location.
  • Age: 24 to 35 years.
  • Physically, mentally, and medically fit with excellent eyesight.
  • Specific physical deployments may require minimum height requirements depending on the client site and operational role.
  • Minimum KCSE mean grade of D+ and above.
  • Ability to read, write, and communicate confidently in English and Kiswahili.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.
Installation Technicians CCTV, access control, alarms, structured cabling, app setup, and client handover.
Project-based, depending on installation demand and site scope.
  • Proven experience installing CCTV, access control, and smart alarm systems.
  • Basic understanding of IP networks, routers, and mobile app integrations.
  • Clean installation standards with neat cabling and proper device placement.
  • Good client communication during testing, training, and handover.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.
Security Solutions Consultants Technical sales, site surveys, client advisory, and security proposal support.
Performance-driven structure with earning potential based on results.
  • Proven B2B, real estate, security, or technical sales experience.
  • Ability to generate leads and follow up professionally.
  • Ability to learn and explain CCTV, alarms, access control, monitoring, and guarding frameworks.
  • Confidence conducting physical site surveys and identifying security gaps.
  • Valid Certificate of Good Conduct required.

Application Documents

Required documents for applicants.

If you meet the qualifications and wish to be considered for future deployment or project opportunities, prepare clear scanned copies of the required documents.

01

A handwritten application letter addressed to the Human Resource Manager.

02

Original National ID Card or Kenyan Passport.

03

Valid Certificate of Good Conduct.

04

Original K.C.S.E and School Leaving Certificates.

05

An introduction letter from your Local Chief matching the details on your National ID.

06

Contacts of three referees, including written letters, phone contacts, and photocopies of their IDs.

07

Reference letters from former employers where applicable.
