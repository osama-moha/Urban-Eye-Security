FAQs

Common retail security questions.

Retail security must protect sales, staff, stock, and the storefront without interrupting daily customer flow.

Can we restrict which staff members can arm or disarm the shop?

Yes. You can assign unique permissions to managers or staff, restrict sensitive areas, and review who opened or closed the store.

How do you prevent false alarms when staff arrive in the morning?

We configure entry delays, staff PINs or tags, and practical opening procedures so authorized staff can disarm the system correctly before an alarm triggers.

What happens if someone smashes the window at night?

Glass-break detection can trigger sirens and alerts immediately. CCTV or visual verification helps confirm whether it is a real incident so the right response can follow.
