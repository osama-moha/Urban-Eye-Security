The Security Gap

Your glass display is your biggest asset, and your greatest vulnerability.

Retailers face multiple risks at the same time: nighttime smash-and-grab attacks, blind spots around shelves and stockrooms, POS disputes, internal shrinkage, and staff safety concerns during business hours. If cameras, alarms, access control, and staff procedures are not working together, you may only be recording your own losses instead of preventing them.

FAQs

Common retail security questions.

Retail security must protect sales, staff, stock, and the storefront without interrupting daily customer flow.

Can we restrict which staff members can arm or disarm the shop?

Yes. Using tools like Ajax KeyPad Plus and encrypted tags, you can assign unique permissions to managers or staff and review who opened or closed the store.

How do you prevent false alarms when staff arrive in the morning?

We configure entry delays, staff PINs or tags, and practical opening procedures so authorized staff can disarm the system correctly before an alarm triggers.

What happens if someone smashes the window at night?

Glass-break detection can trigger sirens and alerts immediately. MotionCam or CCTV verification helps confirm whether it is a real incident so the right response can follow.
