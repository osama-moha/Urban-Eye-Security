FAQs

Common questions.

Where can access control be used?

It can be used at offices, gates, stores, restricted rooms, clinics, warehouses, and staff-only areas.

Can access control replace guards?

Not always. It often supports guards by making entry rules clearer and more controlled.

Do you help choose the right device?

Yes. The right option depends on traffic, users, risk level, and property layout.
