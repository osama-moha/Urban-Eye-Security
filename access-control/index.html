The Security Gap

Uncontrolled keys and shared codes leave you completely blind.

Physical keys can be lost, stolen, copied, or passed around without your knowledge. In offices, estates, clinics, shops, and warehouses, shared PIN codes and manual visitor logs make it difficult to prove who entered a sensitive area and when. Without a digital access record, your property remains vulnerable to tailgating, internal theft, unauthorized entry, and weak accountability.

The Urban Eye Solution

Next-generation access. Secure, trackable, and keyless.

Urban Eye designs access control around real movement: staff entry, visitor routes, restricted rooms, estate gates, parking barriers, stockrooms, and high-risk doors. The goal is simple: the right people, the right places, at the right time.

FAQs

Common access control questions.

Good access control should improve security without making everyday movement frustrating for approved users.

Can I give temporary access to contractors or cleaning staff?

Yes. Temporary access codes or credentials can be configured for specific days, time windows, users, or areas. Once no longer needed, access can be disabled without changing physical locks.

Can access control systems control our main estate gate or warehouse barrier?

Yes. Using suitable relays and smart controllers, selected gates, barriers, turnstiles, roller shutters, and controlled doors can be integrated into a central access-control plan.

What happens to electronic locks if the power goes out?

Enterprise access-control planning should include backup power and fail-safe or fail-secure design. This helps protect assets while ensuring emergency exits remain compliant with life-safety requirements.
