The Security Gap

Traditional alarms are loud, blind, and vulnerable.

A property can look secure, but traditional alarms often leave you without enough context to assess the event. Wires can be cut, power interruptions can create exposure, and false alarms caused by pets or environmental triggers can lead to panic and wasted dispatches. When a siren rings without visual verification, you do not know whether it is a real emergency or just another false trigger.
