The Security Gap

Traditional alarms are loud, blind, and vulnerable.

A property can look secure, but traditional alarms often leave you guessing. Wires can be cut, power interruptions can create exposure, and false alarms caused by pets or environmental triggers can lead to panic and wasted dispatches. When a siren rings without visual verification, you do not know whether it is a real emergency or just another false trigger.

FAQs

Common Ajax alarm questions.

Smart alarms should be easy to control, reliable during interruptions, and clear enough to reduce false responses.

Is a wireless alarm reliable for a large home or business?

Yes. Ajax Jeweller provides secure encrypted wireless communication with long-range coverage suitable for many homes and commercial spaces, with range-extension options for larger sites.

What happens if the power or internet goes out?

Ajax hubs include backup power and can use multiple communication channels depending on the model and setup, helping alerts continue during internet or power interruptions.

Can the alarm system work with physical guards or response teams?

Yes. This supports Urban Eye’s Smart Protection. Human Response approach. Ajax alerts can help guards, monitoring teams, or response personnel know what triggered the alarm and where action is needed.
