The Security Gap

Traditional alarms are loud, blind, and vulnerable.

A property can look secure, but traditional alarms often leave you guessing. Wires can be cut, power interruptions can create exposure, and false alarms caused by pets or environmental triggers can lead to panic and wasted dispatches. When a siren rings without visual verification, you do not know whether it is a real emergency or just another false trigger.

The Urban Eye Solution

Detect, verify, and control.

We design Urban Eye Smart Intrusion architectures around your actual property risks: vulnerable doors, windows, corridors, stores, offices, and after-hours zones. The result is an integrated setup that helps you know what happened, where it happened, and what action should follow.

Intelligent Detection Capabilities

Built for clarity, not panic.

The strongest alarm system is not the loudest one. It is the one that helps you verify the threat and respond correctly.

Visual Alarm Verification

Using advanced motion detectors with built-in cameras, the system sends an encrypted series of photos to your phone the second motion is detected, helping you visually confirm if an alarm is real before taking action.

Reliable Wireless Technology

Our systems use enterprise-grade, encrypted wireless protocols designed for secure long-range communication, anti-jamming protection, and battery life that lasts for years without messy cabling or drilling into your walls.

Pet Immunity & Smart Detection

Advanced detection logic filters out pets and environmental noise, drastically reducing frustrating false alarms while still responding instantly to human movement in protected zones.

Centralized Smartphone Control

Arm, disarm, check system status, view event logs, and receive instant push notifications from anywhere using one intuitive mobile application.
