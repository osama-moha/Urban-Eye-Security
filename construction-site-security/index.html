The Construction Security Gap

Construction sites change daily, but theft only needs one weak night.

Construction sites change daily, making traditional wired security difficult to maintain. Relying only on a single night guard leaves millions of shillings in materials, tools, copper wire, fuel, and heavy machinery vulnerable to organized theft in unmonitored blind spots.

An alarm making noise on an empty site at 3 AM does not stop a coordinated break-in unless the threat is verified and escalated quickly.

The Urban Eye Solution

Temporary, scalable, and proactive protection.

We deploy wireless, battery-backed threat detection systems that move as your building phases evolve. When intrusion occurs after hours, Urban Eye supports visual verification, site escalation, and coordinated response so your materials are not left unguarded.
