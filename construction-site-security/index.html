The Construction Security Gap

Materials disappear when access and exit control are weak.

Relying purely on an unmonitored night guard at the gate leaves your site vulnerable. Without strict Items Entry and Exit Control, cement, steel, tools, copper wire, fuel, equipment, and fittings can leave the site without proper verification.

A single roving guard also cannot patrol a massive, dark site without leaving blind spots. Construction security must combine post guards, patrol routines, access logs, material permits, and perimeter detection.

The Urban Eye Solution

A complete Construction Security Plan.

Urban Eye integrates PSRA-registered guards at the main gate to enforce strict visitor and material logs, supported by wireless, battery-powered perimeter alarms. If an intruder breaches the fence line during patrol hours, the threat can be verified and escalated instead of leaving the site exposed.
