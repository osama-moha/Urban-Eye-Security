The Problem

Commercial fire safety is stuck in the past.

For decades, protecting a commercial building meant accepting major disruption: walls opened, long cable runs, outdated control panels, and systems that operate separately from your cameras and alarms. When a crisis happens, isolated systems waste critical seconds, while false alarms from dust, steam, or poor placement interrupt operations.

The Solution

The All-Hazard Unified Architecture.

Urban Eye connects fire detection, intrusion alarms, CCTV, access control, and emergency automation into one practical safety architecture for commercial buildings.

Smart DetectionUse precision smoke and heat detection designed to reduce unnecessary disruption from common false-alarm triggers.
Synchronized AlertingTrigger loud audio and visual alerts across the facility so alarms are heard and seen in busy or noisy environments.
Emergency AutomationConnect fire alerts to emergency routines such as unlocking exits, managing ventilation, or activating escalation workflows.
